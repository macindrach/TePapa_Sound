Explore Te Papa further with Te Papa Sound

This mobile app provides free audio tours around the museum. Feel free to move at your own pace. Feedback is encouraged.

Step 1: Open the app and ensure that bluetooth is switched on

Step 2: Choose your exhibit

Step 3: Put in your headphones and enjoy your guided tour!

How does it work?

Te Papa Sound utilises Beacon Technology... The bluetooth signals connect with your phone within a metres range, allowing for accuracy while exploring our exhibits.
