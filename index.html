Get more out of your visit with Te Papa Sound

This mobile app provides free audio tours around the museum. Explore our wide range of exhibitions, gaining more knowledge with each step. Without the pressure of keeping up with a guide, you can feel free to move at your own pace. Feedback is encouraged.

Step 1:

Open the app and ensure that bluetooth is switched on.

Step 2:

Choose your exhibit and give our network permission to access your phone.

Step 3:

Wander up to an exhibit piece to hear more about it. We recomend the use of headphones so as not to bother other guests.

How does it work?

Te Papa Sound utilises Beacon Technology. Beacons are placed near exhibit pieces and are activated as you approach. The bluetooth signals connect with your phone within a few centre-metres to a few metres range, and allowing for accuracy while exploring even our tightest exhibits. Our network uses The beacon devices are also useful data collectors: capable of recording time users spend at each exhibit. This allows us to better tailor our exhibits around our audience.
